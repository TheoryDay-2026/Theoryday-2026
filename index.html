NYCU Theory Day 2026 is a two-day in-person event held at the National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU), Hsinchu Campus. The event aims to bring together researchers in theoretical computer science to exchange ideas, present recent advances, and foster collaboration between international and domestic communities.

The NYCU Hsinchu (Chiaotung) Campus is located adjacent to Taiwan’s major science park and semiconductor industry hub. This will be the first time the Theory Day event is hosted at NYCU.
