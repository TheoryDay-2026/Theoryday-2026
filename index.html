NYCU Theory Day 2026

The 2026 NYCU Theory Day event will be held on April 22–23 at the National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU), Hsinchu Campus, Taiwan. The NYCU Hsinchu (Chiaotung) Campus is located adjacent to Taiwan’s major science park and semiconductor industry hub.

The primary objective of NYCU Theory Day is to provide a forum for international and domestic researchers in theoretical computer science to exchange ideas, to expose NYCU students to forefront research in theory, and to encourage young researchers to pursue advanced study in the field. This will be the first time we hold the Theory Day event at NYCU.

Date: April 22–23, 2026
Location: NYCU Hsinchu (Chiaotung) Campus, Taiwan
Format: In-person
